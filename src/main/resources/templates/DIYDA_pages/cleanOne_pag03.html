Cleaning/tidying the data (part 1).

Cleaning the data is a very important operation in order to be able to analyze it.

Many functions used in DA have problems with missing values. Non-standard times and dates are very difficult to operate on,

and erroneous values (e.g. negative ages) can spoil an analysis. In the present version of our app we are not tidying the data.

We are going to divide the cleaning in two sections: 1) dealing with missing values, and 2) the rest.

Before we start our job is necessary to know the spread and structure of the missing data.
